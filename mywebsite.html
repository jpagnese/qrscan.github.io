Words from the Current Owners

We’ve called 1213 Cordova Rd home for many wonderful years, raising our four children in this warm, welcoming space. From first steps to birthday parties to quiet evenings by the fire, every corner of this house holds a memory. It’s a place filled with laughter, love, and the kind of comfort that only comes from truly living in a home.

The large, open living areas have been perfect for hosting family gatherings and holidays, while the spacious backyard with the pool and grill became our go-to space for weekly barbecues and summer celebrations. The upstairs suite, with views of the intracoastal and its peaceful balcony, offers perfect accommodations to a visiting couple.

One of the things we’ve most enjoyed is the unbeatable location. We’re just a short stroll or bike ride from some of Fort Lauderdale’s best local restaurants, cafés, and shops. Everything we needed is just blocks away. And of course, the beach, only about a 7-minute drive, is always there for spontaneous weekend outings or peaceful early morning walks along the shore.

We've made thoughtful upgrades over the years—impact windows, a smart home system, renovated baths—not just for value, but because we truly cared about the comfort and functionality of our everyday life. The neighborhood, nestled between Harbordale Elementary and the Lauderdale Yacht Club, has offered the safety, walkability, and community every parent hopes for.

Now, as we enter the next chapter, we hope the next owners find as much happiness here as we have. This house isn’t just a structure, it’s a story, and we can’t wait to pass the torch to someone who will write the next one.
